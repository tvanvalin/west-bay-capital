## Typography Direction

### Current Fonts
- **Headings:** Raleway (400, 700) — a geometric sans-serif
- **Body:** Noticia Text (400, 700) — a transitional serif designed for body text
- **Fallbacks:** Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif

### Typography Assessment
Raleway is on the **banned font list** (per rebuild prompt design rules). The pairing of Raleway + Noticia Text is functional but generic. The site uses ALL CAPS extensively for section headers and team names, creating a slightly cold, institutional feel.

### Typography Direction for Rebuild
The logo wordmark "WEST BAY" uses a **classic, high-contrast serif** with elegant terminals — reminiscent of Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond. The rebuild typography should:
- **Display/Heading font:** A refined, high-contrast serif that harmonizes with the logo's serif character. Candidates: **Cormorant Garamond**, **Playfair Display**, **DM Serif Display**, or **Libre Caslon Display**.
- **Body font:** A clean, modern sans-serif with good readability at 16px+. Candidates: **DM Sans**, **Plus Jakarta Sans**, **Outfit**, **Figtree**.
- Avoid: Inter, Roboto, Arial, Helvetica, Space Grotesk, Lato, Open Sans, Source Sans Pro (all banned).

## Visual Tone & Personality

### Current Site Personality Keywords
- Conservative, institutional, traditional
- Blue-collar finance, no-nonsense
- Slightly dated, Wix-template feeling
- Trust-oriented but lacking warmth

### Target Design Personality for Rebuild
**"Refined authority with modern sophistication"**

The rebuild should evoke:
- **Authority** — This is a $15M+ commercial lender. The design must convey serious financial capability.
- **Sophistication** — Elevate from "Wix template" to "boutique investment firm." Think private wealth management, not strip-mall loan broker.
- **Confidence** — Bold typography, strong visual hierarchy, decisive color usage. No timidity.
- **Warmth through trust signals** — The testimonials and team photos are strong assets. Elevate their presentation dramatically.
- **Speed & capability** — "Close in 9 business days" is the brand promise. The site should feel fast, decisive, capable.

### Design Aesthetic Direction
**"Luxury editorial meets institutional authority"** — Think: a financial magazine spread meets a premium private equity firm's digital presence. Deep navy anchors the palette. A warm accent color (possibly copper/amber/gold — `#C8954C` range) replaces the generic medium blue to create distinction and luxury signaling. Clean editorial layouts with generous whitespace, dramatic section transitions, and confident typography.

### Differentiation (The Unforgettable Element)
The site's single most memorable element should be the **lending criteria section** — transformed from a plain table into a striking, interactive showcase that immediately communicates "we fund $1M-$15M deals, fast." This is the heart of the business and should be the visual centerpiece.
